Welcome to ShareBuddy!

In this game you must share your real life resources with your buddy , who wants to earn mood points by carrying out daily activities. The player with the most mood points wins the game!

Each activity costs either electricity or water . You can select one activity per day from each of these four categories: cooking, leisure, fitness, and hygiene. To complete an activity, you play a short mini-game.
Each day of the game, we compare how much electricity and water you actually used the day before to your typical usage. Any reduction from your typical usage is shared with your buddy, e.g., a 10% electricity reduction equals 10 electricity points.
The electricity we use every day can be generated in different ways, which have different environmental impacts. For example, wind power pollutes much less than burning coal. This mix of generation means that there are some hours of the day in which electricity is "greener" than other hours.
Another way to earn electricity points is by shifting your electricity use to the greener hours of the day. You can see a 24 hour forecast showing the best and worst hours for using electricity on the Resources page in the game:



You can also see the electricity forecast for the next 3 hours on the main game screen

How to play

How To Play

Once again the stupid moles stole all your power bars. Only tap the moles that have power bars to reclaim what is yours, but be quick as they only appear for a short time! The game is over if you miss more than 5 moles.

Rules have changed! Help the thirsty frog catch the falling rain drops by tapping either side of the screen to move it left or right. The angry bee shoots lightning bolts at the frog draining your health points. The frog can counter-attack by shooting water drops, but doing so costs 3 points and only affects the bee for a little while. If the bee strings the frog, the game is over.

The circuit board is broken and you must fill the gaps to get the spark across by tapping (not dragging) the four corners of the screen to move the two pads up & down. Sparks can jump from one track to another at the yellow line, so be careful! The game is over if you miss more than 5 sparks.

How To Play

The eggs need to fry for 5 seconds to be perfectly cooked. Tap them when you think they are done. If you tap an egg before it is done, you will earn as many points as the number of seconds it has been frying. So a perfectly cooked egg will earn you 5 points, but if you let it fry too long you will earn 0 points!
